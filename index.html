ZIMA Story

ZIMA originated in Japan and was later introduced to the U.S. market in the early 1990s.

In 1994, 1.2 million barrels of the beverage were sold. It was originally popular among young women.

However, despite Zima’s early success, sales began to decline in subsequent years.

The new brand's style wants to present a colorful, youthful and joyful atmosphere.

Therefore, the new label was designed with bright colors.

There are different flavors of fruits in the background, and the labels of each flavor are different colors, emphasizing the uniqueness of each flavor.
